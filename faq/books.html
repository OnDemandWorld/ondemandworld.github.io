01 · OVERVIEW

The Basics — What Is Books?

9 questions

Books is an AI bookkeeper that helps close the month. It automates routine bookkeeping tasks while keeping all financial data on your infrastructure.

Here's how it works:

  1. Import transactions: Pull bank feeds (CSV, OFX, QFX, or API integrations via Plaid/Teller)
  2. AI categorization: AI categorizes transactions based on your chart of accounts
  3. Reconciliation: AI matches bank feed items to ledger entries
  4. Anomaly detection: AI flags duplicates, unusual amounts, missing documentation
  5. Human review: You review and approve AI suggestions
  6. Month-end reports: Generate P&L, balance sheet, cash flow, trial balance

The key difference from other bookkeeping tools: self-hosted, human-in-the-loop, suite-integrated. Your financial data stays on your infrastructure, and no ledger-affecting decision is made without human approval.

Bookkeeping is simultaneously critical and operationally painful:

  • Manual bookkeeping is slow: SMBs spend 10-30 hours per month on routine tasks (categorization, reconciliation, anomaly review)
  • Existing AI tools are cloud-only: Zeni, Truewind, Docyt, Bench, Pilot, Xero AI, QuickBooks AI all require sending financial data to third-party servers
  • Global complexity: Multi-currency, multi-entity, cross-border tax treatment remain painful
  • Trust gap: AI-generated journal entries without audit trails or human oversight create compliance risk

Books solves this by providing self-hosted, human-in-the-loop bookkeeping automation with full audit trails.

Books is designed for SMBs and global founders in regulated industries:

  • Healthcare: Clinics, health tech — HIPAA-adjacent workflows
  • Legal: Law firms — attorney-client privilege
  • Defense: Government contractors — strict data sovereignty
  • Fintech: Financial services — strict compliance
  • Privacy-conscious founders: Global founders who want data control
  • Any SMB: That wants bookkeeping leverage without sending financial data to the cloud

If you can't afford a compliance failure in your bookkeeping, Books is for you.
